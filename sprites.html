AX-50 (Scout-Class Exo-Bot)

Designation: AX-50

Nickname: Sprites

Capabilities:

  • Height: 6 feet
  • Purpose: Light reconnaissance, resource collection, low-risk combat missions
  • Features: Modular arms, small-scale weaponry, group coordination
  • Defenses: Lightweight armor, agile and swift

History:

Initially developed to minimize human risk in hazardous environments, the AX-50 became a cost-effective staple for lower-risk operations. Its swarm tactics make it versatile and efficient in groups of three to five.
