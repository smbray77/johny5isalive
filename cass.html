Casey "Cass" Miller

Nickname: Cass

Role: Mid-level Engineer and AI Creator

Personality: Strong yet approachable; confident without ego; deeply empathetic with a dry sense of humor.

Background

  • Humble Beginnings: Grew up in a small industrial town with a mechanic father and teacher mother, fostering technical curiosity and a strong work ethic.
  • Education and Early Struggles: Balanced practical engineering talent with challenges in theory-heavy courses while working multiple jobs to fund her education.
  • Connection with Elias: Met Elias during their first year of college; collaborated on projects, combining his visionary ideas with her practical solutions. Their friendship grew from late-night brainstorming sessions and mutual respect.
  • Career at Aeternum Industries: Known for practical problem-solving and integrity; earned respect for her ability to "fix the unfixable" but occasionally clashed with the profit-driven corporate culture.

Traits

  • Strengths: Practical thinker, calm under pressure, innovative, loyal.
  • Weaknesses: Overly self-reliant, skeptical of authority, underestimates her impact.
  • Values: Integrity, reliability, community over ambition.

Key Role in Story

Creator of the AI protagonist and among the first to suspect the portal's true nature. Her moral compass and determination drive her to investigate and act, even at personal risk.
