Grolm

Nickname: None

Role: Protagonist; Outcast Troll and Aspiring Party Leader

Personality: Patient, resourceful, and surprisingly intelligent. Grolm is kind-hearted and deeply curious, though his blunt demeanor often masks his softer side.

Background

  • Origins: Grolm was born in the shadow of a mountain and raised in the harsh wilderness of PX-237. Unlike his brutish kin, he quickly developed a knack for observation and strategy, setting him apart as an outlier.
  • Outcast: Grolm’s intelligence and patience were seen as weaknesses by other trolls, who drove him away from their territory. Alone, he learned to rely on his wits and resilience to survive.
  • Discovery of Heroes: Grolm found tattered books in a ruined settlement, teaching him about “heroes” and “parties.” These stories inspired his dream of forming a party, believing that teamwork could conquer the blighted lands.
  • Search for Companions: Despite his fearsome appearance, Grolm seeks allies who will join his quest. Meeting Sunny marks the beginning of his journey to create a true party and prove that even a troll can be a hero.

Traits

  • Strengths: Physical power, strategic thinking, survival instincts, and surprising emotional intelligence.
  • Weaknesses: Limited formal knowledge, mistrusted due to his appearance, and occasionally too idealistic about teamwork.
  • Values: Loyalty, cooperation, and the belief that everyone has a role to play in a party.

Key Role in Story

  • Party Leader: Grolm’s quest to build a party adds humor and heart to the story. His belief in teamwork contrasts with his solitary life as an outcast.
  • Foil to Sunny: Grolm’s earthy wisdom and faith in Sunny’s sentience challenge the AI’s logical worldview, creating a compelling dynamic.
  • Guide to PX-237: Grolm’s experience surviving the blighted lands makes him an invaluable ally, offering unique insights into the world Sunny cannot immediately understand.
