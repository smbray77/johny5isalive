Drokhan (Drok)

Basic Information

  • Full Name: Drokhan
  • Aliases/Nicknames: Drok
  • Role in the Story: Supporting Character, Companion to Kaelen, Future Member of Grolm’s Team
  • Affiliation: Kingdom Army Infantry
  • Current Status: Alive

Appearance

  • Species/Race: Human
  • Gender: Male
  • Age: Late 20s to early 30s
  • Height: Approximately 6’4”
  • Build: Broad-shouldered, muscular
  • Hair: Short and dark
  • Eyes: Deep brown
  • Other Features: Skin is a dark golden hue, weathered by the harsh sunlight of his homeland. A jagged scar on his left forearm from an old sparring accident, which he tells exaggerated stories about.

Personality

  • Traits: Charismatic, arrogant, action-oriented, fiercely loyal
  • Strengths: Fearless in battle, physically strong, quick to bond with others
  • Flaws/Weaknesses: Overconfident, impatient with strategy, brash
  • Core Motivation: Seeks adventure, glory, and camaraderie
  • Fears: Failing to protect friends, losing purpose as a warrior

Background

  • Origin: Born in the eastern regions of the continent
  • Family: Parents were farmers; youngest of four siblings
  • Education/Training: Enlisted in the Kingdom Army; trained alongside Kaelen
  • Significant Events: Fought in numerous skirmishes; defended the breached wall during a recent UM attack

Role in the Story

  • First Appearance: Chapter 3
  • Major Contributions: Joins Kaelen on a mission to investigate UMs near a mountain pass; becomes a key member of Grolm’s team
  • Alliances/Relationships: Best friends with Kaelen; grows to trust Grolm and Elyndra
  • Conflicts: Clashes with those who rely on strategy or intellect over action; learns to trust others’ methods

Abilities/Skills

  • Physical Abilities: Exceptional strength and endurance; agile in close-quarters combat
  • Technical Skills: Proficient with various melee weapons; adept at survival skills
  • Unique Talents: Naturally inspires and rallies others in tough situations
  • Limitations: Relies heavily on brute force; can be provoked into rash decisions

Equipment

  • Weapons: Well-worn greatsword; sturdy dagger
  • Tools/Gear: Practical armor; protective charm from his homeland

Quotes

  • "Why think so hard? The enemy’s right there—hit ‘em!"
  • "Feathers here thinks too much. Me? I just let the blade do the talking."

Fun Facts

  • Trivia: Calls Kaelen “Feathers” due to his lighter complexion and elven features; known for telling exaggerated stories about his exploits.

Notes

  • Drok’s eventual integration into Grolm’s team will highlight his growth, particularly in learning to balance action with strategy and appreciating the strengths of his unlikely allies.
