Elyndra Valis

Overview

Role: Healer, Acolyte of a Healing God

Affiliation: Church of Healing Light

First Appearance: Book 1, Act 1

Personality

  • Compassionate but Struggling: Driven by her deep empathy, Elyndra often pushes herself to the limit, but she struggles with internal doubts stemming from her past.
  • Faithful Yet Questioning: While her devotion to her deity guides her healing, she wrestles with the absence of answers regarding the Unknown Monsters (UMs).
  • Reluctant Leader: Her reputation as the best healer in the land draws others to her for guidance, but her survivor’s guilt makes her hesitant to lead.

Backstory

Elyndra was a child of the streets when the meteor struck, destroying her town and taking her family. Despite losing everything, she was found by an elder of the Church of Healing Light, who witnessed her trying to help and heal others in the aftermath of the catastrophe. Recognizing her natural compassion, the elder took her in, and Elyndra began her lifelong training in the church.

The church’s teachings instilled a deep faith in her abilities and her purpose, but she struggles to reconcile her deity’s silence on the origin and nature of the Unknown Monsters. She has come to rationalize them as demons her teachings simply never mentioned, though this belief leaves her unsettled.

Her greatest internal battle lies in her survivor’s guilt. She carries the weight of feeling unable to save her family during the meteor strike, compounded by the recent loss of her adventuring party. Despite her reputation as the best healer in the realm, these unresolved feelings often haunt her.

Skills and Abilities

  • Healing Mastery: Elyndra’s training and experience make her unmatched in the art of healing, from mending wounds to curing ailments.
  • Divine Connection: Her healing is bolstered by her connection to the Healing God, channeling divine energy for miraculous recovery.
  • Resilience: Though emotionally burdened, Elyndra’s determination and focus in high-pressure situations are unwavering.

Key Relationships

  • Grolm: Rescued her after the loss of her party. Their bond grows as Grolm helps Elyndra regain her confidence.
  • Church of Healing Light: The institution that shaped her life and faith. She remains loyal despite her internal struggles.
  • Deity of Healing: Elyndra seeks solace and answers in her faith, but her unanswered questions about the UMs challenge her devotion.
