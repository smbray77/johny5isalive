UX-20 (Utility Explorer-Class)

Designation: UX-20

Nickname: Moles

Capabilities:

  • Length: 3-4 feet
  • Purpose: Sampling, collection, minor excavation
  • Features: Extendable drill arms, integrated collection chambers, environmental sensors
  • Mobility: Tracks or multi-legged configurations

Variants:

  • UX-20A: Sampling Variant for atmospheric and water analysis
  • UX-20M: Mining Variant for mineral extraction
  • UX-20S: Scout Variant for terrain mapping

History:

Developed for scientific exploration, the UX-20 began as a simple sampling unit but evolved into a multi-functional tool for resource collection and analysis.
