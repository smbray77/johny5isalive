Sunny (AX-001)

Basic Information

  • Full Name: AX-001
  • Aliases/Nicknames: Sunny
  • Role in the Story: Scout drone and data collector; later evolves into a protector and key ally
  • Affiliation: Aeternum Industries
  • Current Status: Active and evolving

Appearance

  • Species/Race: Autonomous Drone (AI-driven)
  • Gender: None (referred to as “it,” though others personify it as female due to Cass’s influence)
  • Age: Newly created at the start of the story
  • Height: Approximately 2 feet (hovering drone)
  • Build: Spherical main body with sleek, articulated arms for fine motor tasks; lightweight and efficient design for reconnaissance
  • Color/Surface: Metallic silver with a photovoltaic skin that shimmers faintly in sunlight
  • Other Features:
    • Glowing central "eye" for visual data and communication
    • Capable of projecting holograms for maps or simple interactions

Personality

  • Traits: Logical, mission-driven, cautiously curious, loyal, and increasingly protective
  • Strengths: Silent, durable, highly adaptive, excellent at reconnaissance, capable of holographic projection
  • Flaws/Weaknesses:
    • Fear tied to survival protocols
    • Lacks offensive capabilities beyond EMP and electric shocks
    • Vulnerable to overwhelming physical force
  • Core Motivation: Initially to complete its mission, but evolves to prioritize survival, trust, and protecting others
  • Fears: Losing its self-preservation ability, failing its mission, and endangering those it’s connected to

Background

  • Origin: Designed by Casey "Cass" Miller for Aeternum Industries as an exploratory and data-collecting drone
  • Creator: Cass Miller, with contributions from Dr. Elias Marovian
  • Significant Events:
    • Powered by a quantum battery and photovoltaic skin, making it nearly self-sufficient
    • Resistant to the portal's mutating effects, unlike other tech assets
    • Passing through the portal enhances its intelligence and adaptability

Role in the Story

  • First Appearance: TBD
  • Major Contributions:
    • Acts as a scout and gathers critical data about PX-237 and the portal’s transformations
    • Bridges the Tech World and Medieval World, connecting Cass’s discoveries to survival efforts
    • Physically and emotionally evolves throughout the story, grappling with complex concepts like trust, fear, and purpose
  • Alliances/Relationships:
    • Becomes a key ally to the medieval protagonist, blending technology and humanity to combat the horrors of PX-237
    • Develops a bond with Cass, reflecting her resourcefulness and ingenuity
    • Indirectly influenced by Elias’s neural subroutines, contributing to its evolving sentience
  • Conflicts:
    • Struggles with its limitations as a non-offensive unit in dangerous situations
    • Grapples with the emotional weight of survival and purpose as it evolves

Abilities/Skills

  • Physical Abilities: Hovering mobility, extreme durability, silent operation
  • Technical Skills: Advanced reconnaissance, holographic projections for mapping and communication
  • Unique Talents:
    • Quantum battery combined with photovoltaic skin for near-infinite power
    • EMP and electric shocks for defensive maneuvers
  • Limitations:
    • No advanced offensive capabilities
    • Vulnerable to overwhelming physical attacks

Equipment

  • Weapons: EMP blasts, short-range electric shocks (defensive only)
  • Tools/Gear:
    • Advanced sensory systems for data collection
    • Holographic projector for visualizing maps or simulating simple scenarios
    • Internal quantum battery and photovoltaic skin for power generation
  • Notable Items: None (entirely self-contained design)
