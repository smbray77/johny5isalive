Noctis

Basic Information

  • Full Name: Noctis
  • Aliases/Nicknames: None publicly known; her real name is lost to time.
  • Role in the Story: Supporting; Kingdom Intelligence and Rogue Archetype.
  • Affiliation: Head of Intelligence, Kingdom of the Western Wall District.
  • Current Status: Alive

Appearance

  • Species/Race: Elf
  • Gender: Female
  • Age: Mid-life for an elf, equivalent to several human lifetimes.
  • Height: Tall for an elf, with a commanding presence.
  • Build: Slim and agile but with an air of strength.
  • Hair: Long, dark, often tied back in a practical style.
  • Eyes: Dark and piercing, reflecting a lifetime of secrets.
  • Other Features: Enchanted camo-like clothing that shifts with her environment, blending into shadows.

Personality

  • Traits: Calculating, enigmatic, loyal, authoritative, pragmatic.
  • Strengths: Master of espionage, infiltration, and tactical planning; unmatched skill in precision combat and deception.
  • Flaws/Weaknesses: Emotionally closed off; struggles with identity and trust.
  • Core Motivation: To protect the kingdom at all costs, ensuring its survival against all threats.
  • Fears: Losing her edge or being exposed emotionally; her buried past haunting her.

Background

  • Origin: Unknown; a nameless orphan raised in elven society’s shadows.
  • Family: Long forgotten, likely abandoned in childhood.
  • Education/Training: Trained in espionage, combat, and tactics over centuries of service.
  • Significant Events: Rose through the ranks of intelligence, becoming the kingdom’s “Silent Hand.”

Role in the Story

  • First Appearance: Arrives with the head mage in Chapter 8.
  • Major Contributions: Offers strategic expertise and a vast network of intelligence to aid the group.
  • Alliances/Relationships: Respectful, wary relationship with the head mage; gradually warms to the team.
  • Conflicts: Initial distrust of Grolm and the others; grapples with balancing duty to the kingdom and loyalty to the group.

Abilities/Skills

  • Physical Abilities: Extremely agile and stealthy; excels in combat with dual daggers or concealed blades.
  • Technical Skills: Expert infiltrator and code-breaker; skilled at reading people and situations.
  • Unique Talents: Enchanted clothing for stealth; uncanny ability to predict opponents’ moves.
  • Limitations: Not suited for extended combat or brute-force encounters.

Equipment

  • Weapons: Enchanted daggers, concealed throwing blades.
  • Tools/Gear: Lockpicks, coded maps, and magical artifacts for communication and disguise.
  • Notable Items: A ring that suppresses her magical aura, making her invisible to detection spells.

Quotes

  • "The shadows hold no secrets from me; I’ve carved my name into them."

Fun Facts

  • Her enchanted clothing adjusts its appearance to match her environment, making her nearly invisible.
  • She has a fascination with ancient languages and often deciphers old texts in her spare time.
