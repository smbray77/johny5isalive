Makoen

Basic Information

  • Full Name: Makoen
  • Aliases/Nicknames: Arcane Warden of the Western Barrier (reluctantly)
  • Role in the Story: Supporting; Blight Specialist and Utility Mage
  • Affiliation: Kingdom of the Western Wall District
  • Current Status: Alive

Appearance

  • Species/Race: Human
  • Gender: Male
  • Age: Early 60s (appears older due to his experiences)
  • Height: Average height, with a slightly hunched posture from years of magical strain
  • Build: Lean but sturdy, with hands calloused from years of magical practice
  • Hair: Short, graying hair with a neatly trimmed beard
  • Eyes: Warm brown but often shadowed with fatigue
  • Other Features: Bears faint magical scars across his forearms from overuse of purification spells

Personality

  • Traits: Wise, compassionate, pragmatic, humble, reluctant leader
  • Strengths: Master of protection and utility magic; vast knowledge of the blight and its effects; calm and steady under pressure
  • Flaws/Weaknesses: Reluctant to use offensive magic; carries guilt from past failures; physically strained by years of magical overuse
  • Core Motivation: To protect the kingdom and its people, ensuring the survival of future generations
  • Fears: Failing to stop the blight or protect his companions; the toll his magic takes on him eventually rendering him useless

Background

  • Origin: Born to a farming family near the kingdom’s outskirts, his village was one of the first to suffer from the blight.
  • Family: Lost his parents to blight-related sickness; taken in by a wandering mage who trained him.
  • Education/Training: Trained in arcane magic at the kingdom’s academies, focusing on purification and protection spells.
  • Significant Events: Held off a UM attack on the western barrier single-handedly, earning him the reluctant title of “Arcane Warden of the Western Barrier.”

Role in the Story

  • First Appearance: Arrives in Chapter 8 with Noctis
  • Major Contributions: Provides critical protection and blight expertise to the group; advises on strategy and magical solutions.
  • Alliances/Relationships: Mutual respect with Elyndra, as both are dedicated to healing and protection; a steadying influence on Grolm, often offering alternative solutions.
  • Conflicts: Internal struggle with his reluctance to embrace the “heroic” image others see in him.

Abilities/Skills

  • Physical Abilities: Average physical fitness; relies heavily on magic for survival.
  • Technical Skills: Exceptional knowledge of magical barriers, purification spells, and area-effect magic.
  • Unique Talents: Can sense blight levels and purify small areas; creates large-scale magical shields and healing wards.
  • Limitations: Overuse of magic can cause physical strain and temporary magical depletion; hesitant to use offensive spells unless absolutely necessary.

Equipment

  • Weapons: A plain but enchanted staff that amplifies his protection spells.
  • Tools/Gear: A satchel of magical reagents for purification rituals; a protective talisman that reduces blight exposure.
  • Notable Items: A charred locket from his childhood village, kept as a reminder of his mission.

Quotes

  • “Titles are for courts and ceremonies. Out here, survival is all that matters.”
  • “Magic is not a hammer; it is the mortar that holds the walls together.”

Fun Facts

  • He grows herbs in his spare time, using them for potions and teas.
  • Despite his serious demeanor, he enjoys telling old folktales to lighten the mood.
